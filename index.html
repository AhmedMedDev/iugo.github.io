What can iugo do for you?

What will it cost?

iugo can:

Help you fund your property development project.
Instantly show you just how much you could be eligible to borrow and how much it will cost from a variety of lenders.
Give you a breakdown of indicative loan options in clear and concise language.
Create an Appraisal of your development, which includes valuable analytics on both the project itself and the indicative loan options; so you can decide which ones are best suited for you.
Once you have decided on an option, iugo will connect you directly to the right lender to apply for funding.
iugo is an analytical system designed to give you hard data on the most favourable loan options for you based on a number of metrics such as cost, leverage, return on capital, etc
iugo will ask you some questions about your development project, such as development costs, duration of build, geographical location, etc.
It will then instantly build a Development Appraisal for you and source some indicative funding options while you watch.
If you choose one of the indicative funding options, then iugo will notify the lender and present your Development Appraisal to them for review.
If the lender likes your development project, then they will contact you to take the enquiry forward and work towards completing the loan for you.
It doesn’t cost anything to try it out. You can try it and see what loan options are open to you as many times as you like.
If you decide on a loan option and are commited to funding your project, then you pay an admin fee of £495.
The Admin fee is paid AFTER you receive interest in your development project from a potential lender
If you successfully complete the loan with the Lender, Then iugo receives a procurement fee from that lender
for facilitating your Business with them. Your
£495 fee will then be refunded.
If you do not successfully complete,
then the £495 pays for:
  • creating and providing you with a professional Appraisal of your project;
  • presenting the Appraisal to the lender and for introducing you to the right contact at the lender.
  • Providing you with feedback and advice on how to re-structure the project in order to make it a viable lending proposition
